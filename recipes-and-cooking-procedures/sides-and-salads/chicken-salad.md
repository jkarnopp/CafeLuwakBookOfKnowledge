# Chicken Salad

  
1 ½ lb   Chicken Breast Cubed  
½ lb      Diced Granny Smith Apples sprinkled with lemon juice  
¼ lb      Dried Cherries  
¼ lb      Toasted Almond Slices  
½ cup   Raisins  
¾ cup   Mayo  
1 cup    Sour Cream  
2 tbl      Dijon Mustard  
2 ribs    Celery Diced  
Salt  
Pepper

Blend all ingredients and add salt and pepper to taste.
