# French Onion Soup

* 18 lbs Onion, medium sliced                                          
* ½ cup brown sugar
* 1 ½ cup butter                                                               
* 2 gal beef stock
* 1 gal chicken stock                                                        
* 2 cup sherry wine

salt and pepper to taste

Sauté onions in butter, add beef stock, then salt and pepper to taste.  Cook for 30 minutes.  Finally add sherry, cook for 10 minutes.
