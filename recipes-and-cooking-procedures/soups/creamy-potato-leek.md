# Creamy Potato Leek

* 
  6 bunches leeks                                                            

* ½ lb butter
* 3 T garlic                                                                      
* 2.5 lb zucchini
* 4 T wasabi powder                                                        
* 1 cup flour
* ½ gal heavy cream                                                        
* 1 gal whole milk
* 4 Veg Base cubes                                                         
* 4 lb. potatoes diced
* 1 ½ T pepper                                                                
* 1 ½ T salt
* 2.25 lb corn

Blend.
