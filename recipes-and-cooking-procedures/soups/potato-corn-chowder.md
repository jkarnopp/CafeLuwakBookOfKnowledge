# Potato/Corn Chowder

* 
  3 \# red skin potatoes, 3/4” dice, cook off ¾ way done – drain

* 1 t chopped garlic                                                         
* 1 t garlic salt
* 1 t onion salt                                                                 
* ½ t pepper
* 1 ½ cups chopped onion                                               
* 1 cup diced celery, ¼ dice

Sauté above items to translucent.  Add to potatoes.  Add ½ gal milk bring up to 140.  Add 2 lb frozen corn.
