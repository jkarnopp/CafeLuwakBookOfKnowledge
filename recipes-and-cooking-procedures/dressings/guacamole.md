# Guacamole

*  1 frozen avocado                                                          
* ½ oz. red onions chopped
* 2 tomato slices chopped                                                
* 2 t Lemon Juice
* ½ t Garlic                                                                      
* ½ t Salt
* ¼ t Hot N Horny Sauce                                                  
* ½ t Cilantro \(dried\)
* ½ t Olive Oil

Mash all ingredients together
